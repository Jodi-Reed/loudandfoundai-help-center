# Automating Review Responses

> **Respond to every Google review quickly and professionally — even when your team is busy.**

Responding to Google reviews is one of the most overlooked reputation management activities for small businesses. Google explicitly factors review responses into local search prominence, and customers notice when a business engages with feedback versus ignoring it.

The Loud and Found AI automated review response system handles this for you — posting replies to new reviews automatically, using responses that are personalized, professional, and on-brand.

---

## Why Responding to Reviews Matters

{% hint style="info" %}
Google encourages businesses to respond to reviews and considers engagement as part of your local search prominence score — one of the three factors that determine where you appear in Maps and Search.
{% endhint %}

Responding to reviews signals three things to both Google and potential customers:

- You are an active, engaged business
- You value customer feedback
- You are trustworthy enough to address both praise and concerns professionally

Businesses that respond to reviews consistently outperform those that do not — even when their overall star ratings are similar.

---

## How Automated Review Responses Work

Once a new review is detected on your Google Business Profile, the system triggers a response workflow based on rules you set during configuration.

**You can configure:**

- **Which reviews to auto-respond to** — all reviews, 5-star only, or reviews above a minimum rating
- **Response timing** — immediately, after a short delay, or within a set window
- **Response tone and templates** — professional, warm, casual, or brand-specific language
- **Different responses by rating** — a warm thank-you for 5 stars, a more careful acknowledgment for lower ratings

---

## Response Logic by Rating

| Star Rating | Recommended Approach | Auto-Response |
|------------|---------------------|---------------|
| 5 stars | Warm thank-you, reinforce the positive | ✅ Fully automated |
| 4 stars | Thank-you with subtle invitation to reach out | ✅ Fully automated |
| 3 stars | Acknowledge feedback, invite direct contact | ✅ With human review option |
| 1–2 stars | Empathetic acknowledgment, offer to resolve | ⚠️ Recommend human review before posting |

{% hint style="warning" %}
For negative reviews — particularly 1 and 2-star ratings — we recommend reviewing the AI-generated response before it posts. Low-star reviews often involve specific complaints that benefit from a personal touch. The system can hold these for your approval rather than posting automatically.
{% endhint %}

## What an Automated Response Looks Like

**Example — 5-star review from a customer:**
*"Amazing service — they fixed my AC in under an hour and the technician was so professional. Will definitely call again!"*

**Automated response posted by the system:**
*"Thank you so much for the kind words! We are thrilled the repair went smoothly and that our technician made a good impression. We appreciate your trust and look forward to being your go-to team whenever you need us. — [Business Name]"*

The response is personal enough to feel genuine, fast enough to signal responsiveness, and professional enough to build trust with anyone reading it.
