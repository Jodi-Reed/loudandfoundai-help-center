# How the Automated Review Workflow Works

> **A step-by-step look at what happens from the moment a job is completed to the moment a review appears on your Google Business Profile.**

The Loud and Found AI review request workflow runs entirely in the background. Once it is set up, your team does not need to remember to ask, follow up, or monitor — the system handles all of it automatically.

---

## The Trigger

Everything starts with a trigger event. The system watches for a specific action in your GoHighLevel CRM and fires the workflow automatically when it occurs.

Common triggers include:

- **Appointment marked as completed** in your calendar
- **Invoice marked as paid**
- **Job status updated to done** in your pipeline
- **Form submitted** by a customer after service

You choose which trigger fits your business during setup. Loud and Found AI configures it for you.

---

## The Workflow — Step by Step

### Step 1 — Trigger fires
The customer's appointment, job, or payment is marked complete in your system. The workflow starts immediately.

### Step 2 — SMS sent within 1–2 hours
A personalized text message goes to the customer's phone number on file. The message includes their name, a brief thank-you, and a direct one-tap link to your Google review page. No searching, no copying a URL — one tap gets them there.

{% hint style="info" %}
Research consistently shows that review requests sent within 1–2 hours of service completion have the highest conversion rates. The system is configured to hit that window automatically.
{% endhint %}

### Step 3 — Follow-up reminder at 24 hours
If the customer did not leave a review after the first message, a single gentle reminder is sent the following day. The reminder is brief and friendly — not pushy.

### Step 4 — Reminders stop automatically
Once a review is submitted, the system detects it and stops all further reminders for that customer. They will never be asked twice.

### Step 5 — Review appears on your Google Business Profile
The customer's review is live on Google. Your rating updates, your review count increases, and your local search visibility strengthens over time.

### Step 6 — Your team is notified
You receive an alert when a new review comes in so you can read it and respond — or let the automated response system handle it for you.

## What a Complete Setup Looks Like

| Component | What It Does |
|-----------|-------------|
| Trigger | Watches for the job completion event in your CRM |
| First SMS | Sent 1–2 hours after trigger with direct Google review link |
| Reminder SMS | Sent 24 hours later if no review submitted |
| Review detection | Monitors for new review submission and stops reminders |
| Team notification | Alerts you when a new review is posted |
| Auto-response | Posts a reply to the review on your behalf |

## A Simple Example

A plumbing customer calls after hours. The AI Receptionist books the job. The plumber marks the visit complete at 3pm. At 4:45pm the customer receives a text:

*"Hi Sarah — thanks so much for choosing [Business Name] today! We'd love to hear about your experience. Tap here to leave us a quick Google review: [link]"*

Sarah taps the link, leaves a 5-star review, and the system posts a thank-you reply within the hour. The owner sees the notification, smiles, and moves on to the next job.
