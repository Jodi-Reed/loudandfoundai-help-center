# What Is the Automated Review Request Service?

> **Ask every customer for a Google review — automatically, at exactly the right moment — without your staff lifting a finger.**

The Loud and Found AI Automated Review Request Service helps small businesses collect more Google reviews consistently, without relying on staff to remember to ask. After a completed job, appointment, or payment, the system sends a personalized SMS or email to the customer with a direct link to leave a Google review — no searching required.

Most business owners know they should ask for reviews. Most do not do it consistently. Automation solves that gap by making the ask repeatable across every single customer interaction.

{% hint style="info" %}
Studies show that over 68% of customers will leave a review when asked directly — but the timing matters. The service is designed to reach customers at their highest point of satisfaction, right after a positive experience.
{% endhint %}

---

## Why Google Reviews Matter for Small Businesses

Google reviews are one of the highest-return activities a small business can invest in. They affect three things that directly impact revenue:

- **Local search ranking** — Google uses review volume, recency, and rating as signals when deciding which businesses appear in Maps and local search results
- **Trust and conversion** — Prospects compare businesses by star rating and review count before making contact. More recent, detailed reviews convert more visitors into customers
- **Visibility at the right moment** — A well-reviewed business appears more prominently when a prospect is actively searching for your type of service

{% hint style="warning" %}
Most satisfied customers will not leave a review unless they are asked. Without a consistent system, your best customers walk away without leaving the social proof that helps you win the next one.
{% endhint %}

## What the Service Includes

The Loud and Found AI Automated Review Request Service covers the full review lifecycle:

- **Automated review requests** — SMS or email sent automatically after a trigger event
- **Follow-up reminders** — A gentle second message if no review is left within 24 hours
- **Review monitoring** — New reviews tracked inside your reputation dashboard
- **Automated review responses** — AI-generated replies posted to Google on your behalf
- **Smart workflow logic** — Reminders stop automatically once a review has been submitted so customers are never asked twice

## Who Benefits Most

This service delivers the fastest return for businesses that:

- Complete jobs, appointments, or services on a regular basis
- Rely on local search visibility to attract new customers
- Receive positive feedback verbally but rarely see it turn into written reviews
- Do not have time or staff bandwidth to follow up with every customer manually

Service businesses, clinics, salons, home services, contractors, and local professional offices consistently see the strongest results.
