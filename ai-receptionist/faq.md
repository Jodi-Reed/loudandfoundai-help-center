# AI Receptionist — Frequently Asked Questions

> **Answers to the most common questions about how the Loud and Found AI Receptionist works, what it can handle, and what it cannot.**

---

## General Questions

### Will callers know they are talking to an AI?

That is entirely your choice. Some businesses prefer full transparency and configure the AI to introduce itself as an automated assistant. Others prefer a more seamless experience.

We recommend being transparent — both because it builds trust and because several states have laws requiring disclosure of AI-generated voice calls. Regardless of your choice, the AI is designed to sound natural and helpful. Most callers rate the experience positively when they know it is AI.

### What happens if the AI does not know the answer?

If a caller asks something outside the AI's knowledge base, it will acknowledge that it does not have that information and offer to take a message or transfer to a team member who can help. **It will not make up an answer.**

You can configure a fallback message for specific question types — for example, directing all pricing questions to a dedicated sales line.

### Can the AI handle multiple calls at the same time?

Yes. Unlike a human receptionist, the AI can handle an unlimited number of simultaneous inbound calls. No caller will ever be put on hold or sent to voicemail because all lines are busy.

### Does it work on my existing phone number?

Yes. We can configure the AI Receptionist to work with your current business phone number through call forwarding, or we can provision a new number for you. Most businesses prefer to keep their existing number — your customers already have it.

## Appointments and Bookings

### What calendars does the AI connect to?

The AI Receptionist connects to the GoHighLevel calendar system, which can sync with Google Calendar. During setup we configure your available time slots, service durations, buffer times, and any blackout dates so the AI only books appointments you are actually available for.

### Can callers reschedule or cancel appointments through the AI?

Yes. The AI can look up an existing appointment by phone number and allow the caller to reschedule or cancel. Changes are reflected immediately in your calendar and you receive a notification.

---

## Calls and Escalation

### What types of calls should still go to a human?

The AI handles routine, repeatable calls very well. For the following situations, we recommend configuring a direct transfer to a human team member:

- Complaints or upset customers requiring empathy and judgment
- Complex sales conversations with multiple variables
- Situations requiring sensitive or confidential information
- Any caller who specifically requests to speak with a person

{% hint style="info" %}
The AI will always honor a caller's request to speak with a human and transfer the call immediately — no delay, no pushback.
{% endhint %}

### What if no one is available when the AI tries to transfer a call?

If a transfer is attempted and no one answers, the AI takes a detailed message including the caller's name, number, reason for the call, and urgency level. That message is logged in your CRM and a notification is sent to your team immediately so you can call back promptly.

---

## Data and Privacy

### Are calls recorded?

Call recordings and transcripts are available in your GoHighLevel dashboard. Recording laws vary by state — in some states you are required to inform callers that the call may be recorded. We include an appropriate disclosure in your AI's greeting by default and advise you on your state's specific requirements during setup.

### Where is caller data stored?

Caller information is stored securely within your GoHighLevel CRM account. **You own your data.** Loud and Found AI does not sell or share your customer data with third parties.
