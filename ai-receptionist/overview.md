# What Is the Loud and Found AI Receptionist?

> **A 24/7 virtual front desk that answers calls, qualifies leads, and books appointments — so your business never misses an opportunity.**

The Loud and Found AI Receptionist is a smart, voice-powered assistant that handles inbound phone calls on behalf of your business around the clock. It greets callers, answers common questions, qualifies leads, books or reschedules appointments, and routes calls to the right person — all without requiring a human to pick up the phone.

Built on the GoHighLevel platform, the AI Receptionist integrates directly with your calendar, CRM, and follow-up automation so every interaction is logged and no lead is left behind.

{% hint style="info" %}
The AI Receptionist is designed for small and mid-size businesses that receive repetitive inbound calls, miss leads after hours, or spend too much staff time on scheduling and basic FAQs.
{% endhint %}

---

## What It Does

The AI Receptionist acts like a front-desk assistant that never clocks out. Here is what it handles automatically:

- **Answers routine questions** — hours of operation, services offered, pricing basics, and directions
- **Books, changes, and cancels appointments** — through direct calendar integration, no back-and-forth required
- **Qualifies leads** — asks scripted questions to assess intent and urgency before handing off to staff
- **Routes calls** — transfers urgent or complex calls to the right human person in real time
- **Captures caller information** — name, phone number, needs, and urgency, then pushes it into your CRM
- **Sends confirmations and follow-ups** — automated SMS and email messages after calls or bookings

---

## What Happens After a Call

When the AI Receptionist captures a lead or answers a question, it moves that contact into your workflow automatically. Depending on your setup, the system will:

1. Log the conversation details so your team can review what the caller asked
2. Create or update a contact record in your GoHighLevel CRM
3. Tag the lead, assign it to a pipeline stage, or flag it for follow-up
4. Send an SMS or email confirmation, reminder, or nurture message to the caller
5. Create an appointment on your calendar if the caller booked a time
6. Route to a human agent if the caller requests one or the issue is too complex to handle automatically

**Example:** A plumbing customer calls after hours: "My sink is leaking." The AI Receptionist collects the address, nature of the issue, and preferred appointment time. It books the visit, sends a confirmation text to the customer, and creates a CRM record — all before anyone on your team arrives the next morning.

## AI Receptionist vs. Human Receptionist

| Capability | AI Receptionist | Human Receptionist |
|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Available 24/7 | ✅ Always on | ❌ Business hours only |
| Handles multiple calls at once | ✅ Unlimited | ❌ One at a time |
| Books appointments automatically | ✅ Via calendar integration | Depends on access |
| Logs every interaction to CRM | ✅ Automatically | Manual entry required |
| Handles complex complaints | ❌ Routes to human | ✅ Best for nuanced issues |
| Nuanced sales conversations | ❌ Limited | ✅ Human judgment required |

{% hint style="warning" %}
The AI Receptionist is not a full replacement for a skilled human in every situation. Complex complaints, sensitive customer issues, and high-stakes sales conversations still benefit most from a real person. The AI handles the volume — your team handles the nuance.
{% endhint %}

---

## Who Benefits Most

The AI Receptionist delivers the greatest immediate value for businesses that:

- Receive high volumes of repetitive inbound calls (scheduling, FAQs, directions)
- Miss leads after hours, on weekends, or during busy periods
- Spend significant staff time on basic administrative tasks
- Operate in service industries where speed of response directly affects conversion rates

Service businesses, clinics, salons, home services, and local professional offices tend to see the fastest and most measurable return.
