# Getting Started with the AI Receptionist

> **Everything you need to know before your AI Receptionist goes live — what to prepare, what to expect, and how to get the most out of it from day one.**

Setting up your Loud and Found AI Receptionist is handled by our team — you do not need any technical experience. This article walks you through what to prepare before setup, what happens during onboarding, and how to ensure a smooth launch.

{% hint style="info" %}
Setup is completed by Loud and Found AI on your behalf. Your role is to provide the information your AI Receptionist needs to represent your business accurately. Most businesses are live within 3 to 5 business days of providing complete setup information.
{% endhint %}

---

## Before You Get Started

Gather the following information before your onboarding call. Having this ready speeds up your setup significantly.

### Business Basics
Your business name, address, phone number, website URL, and hours of operation — including how you want after-hours calls handled.

### Services and Pricing
A list of your services and any pricing information you want the AI to share with callers. Include what you do and do not offer so the AI does not overpromise.

### Common Questions
The top 5 to 10 questions your staff answers repeatedly. These become the foundation of your AI's knowledge base and determine how accurately it handles routine calls.

### Escalation Contacts
Who should the AI transfer calls to for urgent issues, complex requests, or callers who specifically ask for a human? Provide names and direct numbers.

---

## Setup Process

### Step 1 — Onboarding Call
We walk through your business details, services, common caller questions, and how you want calls handled. This call typically takes 30 to 45 minutes.

### Step 2 — AI Configuration
Our team builds your AI Receptionist's script, trains it on your services and FAQs, connects it to your calendar for appointment booking, and configures your call routing rules.

### Step 3 — CRM and Automation Setup
We connect the AI to your GoHighLevel CRM so every call is logged, leads are tagged and assigned, and follow-up automations fire correctly after each interaction.

### Step 4 — Testing
We run test calls to verify that the AI greets callers correctly, answers your key questions accurately, books appointments successfully, and escalates to a human when needed.

### Step 5 — Go Live
Your AI Receptionist is activated on your business phone number. From this point forward it handles inbound calls 24/7 according to the rules we configured together.

### Step 6 — Review and Refine
After the first week, we review call logs with you, identify any gaps in the AI's responses, and make adjustments. Fine-tuning is a normal part of the first 30 days.

---

## What to Expect After Launch

Once your AI Receptionist is live, here is what your team will experience:

- **Fewer missed calls** — every inbound call is answered immediately, even after hours and during busy periods
- **CRM notifications** — your team receives alerts when a new lead is captured or an appointment is booked
- **Call logs in your dashboard** — every conversation is recorded and summarized so you can review what callers asked
- **Appointment confirmations** — callers who book receive automatic SMS and email confirmations and reminders
- **Human escalations** — calls that need a person are transferred to the right team member in real time

{% hint style="success" %}
**Pro tip:** Review your call logs during the first two weeks. The most common questions callers ask that the AI does not handle perfectly are your best signal for where to add more detail to the knowledge base. Share those with us and we will update the AI accordingly.
{% endhint %}
